NBLU Website

Introduction

The NBLU website is designed to allow users to register for the 2022 conference and find out more about the purpose of NBLU, the past conference/speakers, and to get a better understanding as to what to expect out of the 2022 conference.

I built this website with the assistance of my coworkers during my time working at 5by5 Agency in the fall of 2021.

In total, this a 10 page website desinged utilizing WordPress and BeaverBuilder within Wordpress. I used BeaverBuilder, PHP, SCSS, Git, BitBucket, and Forge to create this website.

Planning

The planning phase took many months between the combination of directors, client managers, designers, and developers working together.

Result

Reflection

NBLU website is my first professionally built website of my career, and it's one that I'm proud to share.

I worked tirelessly to become a web developer, and 5by5 Agency gave me the opportunity to finally put my skills to the test. The success of the development of this website was very important to me at the time because it was my very first opportunity to prove I could do it. I wanted to prove to myself that all the hard work and long hours would pay off - and they did.

Looking back, it wasn't perfect. There were hurdles and setbacks. Still, the site could be improved upon. However, there was an immense amount of work put in at a team level to produce this website and it shows in my opinion.
